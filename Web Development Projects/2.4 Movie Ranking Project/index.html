The best films according to Suzanne

My top 3 films of all time

A Time to Kill

A gripping courtroom drama where a father takes the law into his own hands to protect his daughter. The film explores themes of justice and morality, leaving viewers questioning the true meaning of right and wrong.

Pretty Woman

A romantic comedy that tells the story of a wealthy businessman who falls in love with a spirited prostitute. Their unlikely relationship challenges societal norms and showcases the transformative power of love.

Bridget Jones's Diary

A charming romantic comedy that follows the life of a single woman navigating her career, friendships, and love life. Bridget's humorous and relatable journey resonates with audiences, making it a timeless favorite.
